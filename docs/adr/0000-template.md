# 0000. Title of the decision

- Status: Proposed
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

The forces at play: the problem, the constraints, what we know. State facts and tensions, not the decision yet. Keep it
honest about trade-offs so a future reader understands why this was a real choice.

## Decision

What we decided, in active voice ("We will ..."). Name the main alternatives considered and why they lost.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder, including the downsides we knowingly accept. Note anything that would
trigger revisiting this decision later.
