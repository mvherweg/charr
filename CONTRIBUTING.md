# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in Charr.

This is a personal project, developed in the open but not run as a collaborative one. To keep it focused:

- It is **not open to external contributions**. Pull requests will not be reviewed or merged.
- It is **not open to issues or feature requests**. The issue tracker is not monitored.

You are welcome to use Charr under the terms of the MIT license (see [LICENSE](LICENSE)) and to fork it for your own
purposes.
